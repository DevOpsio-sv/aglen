# Aglen UI/UX Design

## Experience Principles

1. Make Ъглен feel discoverable, not crowded.
2. Lead with the River Vit landscape and the rare `Ъ` identity.
3. Balance folklore and verified history with clear source notes.
4. Keep tours easy to scan and ready for booking integration.
5. Use premium effects only where they support atmosphere and comprehension.

## Information Architecture

- Hero: emotional arrival, village identity, primary actions.
- Highlights: quick facts for first-time visitors.
- Story: historical and legendary timeline.
- Places: natural and cultural landmarks.
- Experiences: guided services from the Wix source.
- Media: gallery area prepared for owner-exported Wix assets.
- Contact: next-step CTA and source transparency.

## Premium Effects

- Layered hero atmosphere with abstract mountain, mist, and river-line shapes.
- Sticky glass navigation and glass content cards.
- Soft page grid and radial background lighting for depth.
- Hover lift on cards, buttons, media tiles, and navigation.
- Entrance reveal animation with reduced-motion override.
- Image-ready treatments: rounded media frames, saturation/contrast hover, caption structure.

## Accessibility and Performance

- Static rendering and CSS-driven effects keep runtime light.
- `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` disables animation and transition duration.
- Text sits on solid or blurred light surfaces for readability.
- Decorative atmosphere elements use `aria-hidden`.
- Navigation uses real anchors and semantic sections.

## Future UX Enhancements

- Replace media placeholders with exported Wix photos/videos.
- Add a real booking form or Cloudflare Pages Function.
- Add map embeds or GPX route downloads.
- Add bilingual BG/EN content if targeting international tourists.
- Add CMS-backed content if village events and routes change often.
